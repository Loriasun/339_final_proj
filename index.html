About

The Chinese National Women's Volleyball Team (Chinese Women's Volleyball Team) is affiliated to the Chinese Volleyball Association and is one of the sports teams with outstanding performance among all sports teams in China.



The team won the championships in the 1981 and 1985 World Cups, the 1982 and 1986 World Championships, and the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics, becoming the world’s first "Five Consecutive Championships" and in the 2003 World Cup, 2004 Olympics, 2015 He won the World Cup in 2016, the 2016 Olympics, and the 2019 World Cup five times and became world champions for ten times (including the World Cup, World Championships and Olympic Games).



The Chinese women's volleyball team is the only team to win the championship trophy among China's three major goals.
