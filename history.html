History

The Chinese women's team won the 2019 World Cup and successfully defended their champion. This was the Tenth World Championship won by the Chinese women's team. Xi, the general secretary, called to congratulate them and encouraged them to keep high fighting spirit, neither arrogant nor rash, and make more achievements. As the only sports team that had won the world championship in the three major balls, the influence of the women's team in China had already surpassed the meaning of sports itself. It was not only the collective memory of the times, but also the spiritual symbol to encourage the Chinese people to continue to struggle and improve themselves. Although the results have ups and downs, the spirit of the women's team, which is united, cooperative and tenacious, has been passed down from generation to generation, greatly arousing the pride, self-esteem and self-confidence of Chinese people, and providing a strong spirit power for us to advance on our new journey.



After thirty years of hard work, tears welled up in the eyes of several generations. He would never give up and never give up in the face of a strong enemy. Your figures are the microcosm of national characters, and your steps are the growth process of a country. Playing the national song and raising the national flag, you are the heroes of the country, surpassing sports.
